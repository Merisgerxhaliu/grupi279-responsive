About us

jCoders Academy is an extracurricular educational institution, build as an alternative channel to offer practical learning experience in technology for kids and youth starting from age 8. jCoders mission is to offer a learning experience that develops 21st century skills and prepares our young generation for the creative demands of the new reality in the workplace. jCoders is founded in Kosovo in year 2015, and today stands with 6 centers in Kosovo and Albania.
